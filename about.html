About Rebecca

Illustration rooted in emotion, softness, and story

Rebecca Olivia Speed is an illustrator and character artist whose work centers on expressive storytelling, personal visual language, and emotionally led character design.

Her portfolio blends softness, sketch energy, and atmosphere with a strong focus on personality and visual feeling. Whether creating finished illustration pieces or early sketchbook work, the heart of the process stays the same: making work that feels human, intimate, and memorable.

Rebecca is available for selected commissions and creative projects.
