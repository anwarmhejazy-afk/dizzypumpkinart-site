The Artist

About ♡

I’m Rebecca Olivia Speed, an artist focused on illustration, character work, and emotional visual storytelling.

A visual language built around feeling

I create artwork that explores emotion, character, memory, and the quieter parts of human experience.

I’m especially drawn to images that feel personal — pieces that hold softness, tension, mood, and a sense of inner life.

Through illustration, I try to create work that feels alive, expressive, and emotionally honest.

My work is inspired by emotion, identity, people, atmosphere, and the strange beauty of things that are difficult to explain out loud.

I love creating characters and illustrations that feel intimate, soft, expressive, and imperfect in the best way — like fragments of a story caught somewhere between memory and imagination.

I’m interested in the tension between softness and chaos, and I often explore that through line, expression, colour, texture, and mood.
