Custom Artwork

Commissions

If you’re interested in a custom illustration or character piece, here’s a simple overview of the process and what to include in your enquiry.

How it works

  1. Send an enquiry with your idea, references, and intended use.
  2. Rebecca reviews the brief and confirms availability.
  3. Scope, pricing, and timeline are discussed before work begins.
  4. Artwork is developed with updates at agreed stages.
  5. Final files are delivered once the project is complete.

What to include

  • Type of commission
  • Character or subject details
  • Visual references or mood references
  • Preferred deadline
  • Personal or commercial use

Commission notes

  • Pricing varies depending on scope and complexity.
  • Timelines depend on current workload and project size.
  • Clear references always help the process.
  • Commercial work should be stated from the beginning.

Best for

  • Original characters
  • Portrait-inspired illustrations
  • Story-led visual pieces
  • Personal projects and gifts
  • Selected creative commissions
